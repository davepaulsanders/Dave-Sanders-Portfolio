Guitarist and Software Engineer from Washington D.C.

Dave has shared the stage with a diverse range of artists, including Renée Fleming, Patti LuPone, Dionne Warwick, Christopher Jackson, Michelle Williams, and Audra McDonald. He additionally works with many of the areas leading orchestras, including the National Symphony Orchestra, the American Pops (principal), the National Philharmonic, and the Kennedy Center Opera House Orchestra.

Dave has appeared on PBS for several specials, including “Ella Wishes You A Swinging Christmas,” “United in Song,” and the Hispanic Heritage Awards. On the radio, Dave’s version of “America the Beautiful” appeared on NPR Crosscurrents, and various projects have been featured on WRTI FM in Philadelphia. In addition to his performing career, Dave has taught guitar and music theory (Towson University, Rutgers University)
